# Project Context

## Product

Global Staffing Partners (GSP) provides end-to-end recruitment and managed staffing support for companies hiring reliable remote and offshore talent.

## Positioning

**Primary statement:** Global Staffing Partners helps companies hire, manage, and scale high-performing remote talent through seamless recruitment and managed staffing support.

GSP is not positioned as a cheap outsourcing marketplace or resume factory. It is a hands-on recruitment and workforce partner built around visibility, accountability, quality, and ongoing support.

## Business outcomes

- Generate qualified client enquiries and booked calls.
- Explain the recruitment process clearly.
- Build trust before a prospect speaks with GSP.
- Give clients visibility into hiring and contractor performance.
- Give internal teams a reliable operating workflow.
- Give applicants and contractors a clear, respectful experience.

## Audiences

1. Prospective clients: founders, executives, operations leaders, and hiring managers.
2. Existing clients.
3. Applicants and candidates.
4. Independent contractors.
5. GSP HR/recruitment staff.
6. GSP administrators.
7. Finance staff in a later phase.

## Product principles

- Client trust before visual novelty.
- Operational visibility before advanced analytics.
- Simple workflows before automation.
- Security and tenant isolation by design.
- Premium and approachable, never flashy or generic.
- Launch useful functionality early; do not overbuild.

## Departments supported

- Human Resources and Recruitment
- Marketing and lead generation
- Contractor operations
- Product and internal systems
- Finance and billing later

## Success indicators

- Qualified leads can understand the offer and book a call easily.
- Pages work well on mobile and desktop.
- Users see only information allowed for their role and organization.
- Recruitment stages and next actions are always understandable.
- Staff spend less time maintaining disconnected spreadsheets and status updates.
