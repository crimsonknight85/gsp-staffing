# Dashboard Requirements

## Shared standards

Every dashboard includes role-appropriate navigation, organization context, search/filter where useful, clear page titles, status badges, next actions, empty/loading/error states, mobile-safe tables or cards, and no inaccessible data in client-side payloads.

## Client dashboard

- Active job openings and hiring pipeline progress
- Applicants by stage and shortlisted candidates
- Interview schedule/progress and requested feedback
- Assigned contractors and projects
- Tasks, goals, deliverables, blockers
- Hours worked and submitted/approved timesheets
- Weekly/monthly updates and simple reports

## HR dashboard

- Open roles by client
- Candidate pipeline and stage aging
- Applicant profiles, notes, resumes, documents
- Screening results and interview stages
- Client requirements and feedback
- Onboarding checklist
- Overdue actions and upcoming interviews

Sensitive internal notes must be clearly separated from client-visible or applicant-visible notes.

## Applicant dashboard

- Profile completeness
- Resume and permitted documents
- Applied jobs
- Current application status and stage history appropriate to expose
- Next step and interview reminders
- Withdrawal and support/privacy options

Do not expose internal scoring, protected characteristics, confidential notes, or other candidates.

## Contractor dashboard

- Assigned client/project information approved for display
- Tasks, goals, due dates, notes, blockers
- Timer and manual time entry
- Weekly timesheet totals
- Draft/submitted/approved/rejected status
- Rejection reason and resubmission path
- Updates and support contact

## Admin dashboard

- User invitations and access/membership management
- Organization/client management
- Operational overview and exception queues
- Audit views for sensitive actions
- Reference/status configuration only where safe

## Finance dashboard — later

- Hours by client/project/contractor
- Billable vs non-billable hours
- Contractor payout estimates
- Client invoice summaries
- Gross margin with controlled access
- Paid/unpaid status and recurring revenue summaries

Financial figures must state currency, period, source, and whether they are estimates.
